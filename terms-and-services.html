4) 30 Days Warranty rules

30 Days warranty means the following, if an update happen to be released for an item you purchased within the next 30 days of your purchase. You will be emailed the new version. If after these 30 days an update rolls out, you will not be eligible for said updated version. You will need to purchase the item again.
You can not get back your money unless you can prove to me and to paypal that you received nothing after purchasing or if the link is broken of the item you purchased. You will need to contact me about this using the Pack loss inquiry, provide your email of which you paid with (as I can only see this when you completed the transaction) and you will retrieve the item nontheless.

30 Days warranty for collaboration is almost the same. When purchasing a custom made product, you will have 30 days to request small changes to the product without extra costs. However, large updates such as adding a whole new part to the product, or a completely new item to the package, will not be free and you will be charged. As every item is only delivered after complete approval of the customer, updates are rarely needed but you have the rights to request them. There's no guarantee these updates will be executed as they would have to be reviewed first.
